PPG Helper Guide

PPG

PPG stands for Play Point Games and it is a website where you can get PPG points for free and trade them for PEDs, Bitcoins or even Steam and Amazon gift cards.

You can use this link to register: https://playpointgames.com/r/51bfb12f

You have to log in with Google or Facebook.

How to earn PPG Points?

You can win PPG points by playing mini games at the site, watching videos, completing Surveys and Offerwalls, and at the PPG sweat event:

 

PPG Sweat Event

PPG sweat event happens every day at Royal Club 19:00 game time and used to be (and may be once again) a sweat buy program that pays 2ped for 1000 Sweat bottles for each event, with a 0.50 PPG points bonus for those who used the code to register at the website.

For now just the 0.50 PPG points is still active.

They have increased the points to 1 PPG point every event

How to register to the event?

In order to register to the event first you need to Log in and fill your Entropia Universe avatar name at the Settings & Security tab

After doing that you will need the code for the event wich can be found at the PPG Discord page: PPG DISCORD

When you have the code for the event you'll need to insert it at the event page by clicking on the day of the event at the calendar.

 

PPG Games

There are currently 5 games at the site that can give you PPG points. The ones that have "WIN CASH" at the description

One of them is ANTAGONIST WARS. AW is a Solitaire card game that pays up to 2 PPG points each time you complete the game.

The other 4 of them have leaderboards. Each week, only your highest score on each leaderboard counts towards the prize.

And the prizes each week are based on your rank.

So you can make up to 20 PPG points each week on these games.

Watching Videos

Loot.tv is an engaging, rewarding video platform where creators and communities can earn rewards for uploading and interacting with their favorite content.

To redeem LOOT.TV points for PPG points you need to link your PPG account to LOOT.TV

Surveys and Offerwalls

You can also earn PPG points by completing surveys and offerwalls.

Bonus Reward

Members with highest total offerwall withdrawals will be rewarded each month. Current payouts for top 3 positions are 50, 30 and 20 PPG points respectively.
